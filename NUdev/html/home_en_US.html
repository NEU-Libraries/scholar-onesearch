How does this work?

Scholar OneSearch provides simple, one-stop searching for books and e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and more. Search local Northeastern collections and global resources, with many items available in full text online.

Scholar OneSearch also helps you manage your research. Sign in with your myNEU credentials in order to:

  • Renew books and videos
  • Create lists
  • Export citations to Refworks and Endnote Web
  • Order materials from other libraries.
  • View full search results. (Some databases, like Web of Science, only show results when you're signed in.)
What am I searching?
  • All books, e-books, videos, journals, and other collections
  • Articles, proceedings, and digital materials from a variety of licensed and open source databases. If you only want items located at Snell Library, the Law Library, and the African American Institute, limit your search by choosing "Library Catalogs"
  • View a complete list of publishers and providers whose content is included in Scholar OneSearch
 Where can I get help?
  • Ask a librarian how to start your search
  • Dig deeper into library resources with dedicated subject guides, which offer advice, recommendations, and contact information for subject librarians in your discipline.
