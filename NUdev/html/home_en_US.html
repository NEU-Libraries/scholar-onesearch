Welcome to Scholar OneSearch


Scholar OneSearch provides simple, one-stop searching for books and e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and more. Search local Northeastern collections and global resources, with many items available in full text online.

Looking only for books, e-books, or videos located at Snell Library, the Law Library, and the John D. O’Bryant African American Institute? Limit your search by choosing "Library Catalogs” in the dropdown menu.

What am I searching?
  • Books and E-books
  • Scholarly and Peer-Reviewed Articles
  • Newspaper Articles
  • Journals
  • Video and Audio
  • Government Documents

And more from a variety of licensed and open source databases and resources.
Why should I sign in?


Sign in with your myNEU username and password in order to:

  • Renew books and videos
  • Save results in My Favorites
  • Request materials from other libraries via Interlibrary Loan (ILL)
  • Export citations to Refworks and Endnote Web
  • View full search results (Some databases, like Web of Science, only show results when you’re signed in)
