What am I searching?

  • All books, e-books, videos, journals, and other collections from our legacy catalog, NUCat
  • Articles, proceedings, and digital materials from a variety of licensed and open source databases. If you only want items print materials located at Snell Library, the Law Library, and the African American Institute, limit your search by choosing "Library Catalogs" or "Law Library Catalog Only" from the drop-down menu.

Where can I get help?

  • View our Quick Guide (PDF)
  • Ask a librarian how to start your search
  • Dig deeper into library resources with dedicated subject guides, which offer advice, recommendations, and contact information for subject librarians in your discipline.
